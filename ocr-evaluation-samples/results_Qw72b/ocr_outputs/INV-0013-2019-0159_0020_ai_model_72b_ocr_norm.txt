commission des communautes europeennes direction générale des relations extérieures draft reply to the united states ref. consultations germany/airbus the commission of the european communities refers to your government's request for consultations concerning the exchange rate mechanism for deutsche airbus, and informs you that it is prepared to accede to this request. it is suggested to hold these consultations in geneva between the 24 and 27 april 1989. your attention is drawn to the fact that trade in civil aircraft, due to the special factors which apply in that sector, is governed by the gatt agreement on trade in civil aircraft. under these circumstances these consultations should be held under the civil aircraft [redacted] interpreted in the right acceptance of your request does not prejudice the community's position as regards the nature of the abovementioned mechanism, and does not imply that it constitutes an export subsidy or that it is causing or threatening to cause injury to an us industry or otherwise causes serious prejudice to us interests. rue de la loi 200 - b-1049 bruxelles - belgique téléphone: ligne directe 23........ standard 235.11.11 - télex comeu b 21877 - adresse télégraphique comeur bruxelles - télécopieur 235 65 05
