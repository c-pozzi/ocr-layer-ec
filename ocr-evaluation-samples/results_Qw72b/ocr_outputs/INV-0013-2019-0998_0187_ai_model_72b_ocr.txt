The Summary Agenda proposed for the meeting is attached.
Some organisations, have already made available to the Government
of Poland important materials for consideration at the meeting.
We ask that materials which other invitees to the meeting may
wish to have considered be submitted (both to my Ministry in
Warsaw and to the G 24 Coordination Unit in Brussels) no later
than Friday, February 28. This will permit rigorous Polish revie
of the materials and facilitate circulation by the G 24 Coordi-
nation Unit to all external invitees in ample time for their
study before the meeting.

The meeting will convene at 16:00 on Wednesday, March 11, 1992,
at the premises of the Ministry of Labour in Warsaw (1/3 Nowo-
grodzka St.). The Ministry of Labour will provide simultaneous
interpretation between Polish and English for the meeting.
As is normal G 24 practice, invitees from abroad would be
expected to absorb the costs of their attendance and make the
necessary travel and living arrangements.


Sincerely yours,
